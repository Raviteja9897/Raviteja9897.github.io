QT Interval

A Corona Tale

A Short Film

May 4th 2020

QT INTERVAL is a 2020 Indian Telugu Short Film written and directed by Mohana Priya & Shashank.It stars Mohana Priya in a lead role.This tells us the story of a girl who faced some problems in quarantine time.Story goes on how her friends reacted to her in the lockdown situation.Everyone figured in different ways to protect and prevent themselves from corona virus.This picture is specially Encountered with a dog, who helps her find what she need.

Script Writer,Mohana Priya says "Each one has shot their portion of the film in their own homes and then this film has been stitched together. And yet we get the impression that all these people live under the same roof."

Conclusion

COVID-19, A Pandemic that has put the world in a pathetic situation rightnow. This was our small attempt to show various kinds of people facing and managing different situations at home during this lockdwn period. This picture has recorded by everybody staying indoors only. Our main purpose behind making this picture is to state that "A MASK IS A MUST WHILE STEPPING OUT OF THE HOUSE".
